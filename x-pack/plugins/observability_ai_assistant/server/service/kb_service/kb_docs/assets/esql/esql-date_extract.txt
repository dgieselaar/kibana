DATE_EXTRACT
Extracts parts of a date, like year, month, day, hour. The supported
field types are those provided by java.time.temporal.ChronoField
    ROW date = DATE_PARSE("2022-05-06", "yyyy-MM-dd")
    | EVAL year = DATE_EXTRACT(date, "year")
  date:date                  year:long
  -------------------------- -----------
  2022-05-06T00:00:00.000Z   2022
